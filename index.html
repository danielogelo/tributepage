Dr. José Rizal — National Hero of the Philippines

Dr. José Protacio Rizal (1861–1896) was a Filipino nationalist, writer, physician, and revolutionary whose works sparked the Filipino movement for independence from Spain. He authored influential novels such as Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo, which exposed social injustices and inspired generations of Filipinos to fight for freedom. Even in his final moments, Rizal advocated peaceful reform and the empowerment of his people through education, unity, and moral courage.
